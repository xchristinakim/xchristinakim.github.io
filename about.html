Who?

I'm a senior at Northwestern University studying Biomedical Engineering with a minor in Entrepreneurship and a Segal Design Certificate in Product Development. I love building things that sit at the intersection of engineering, health, and human experience.

Beyond the Lab?

When I'm not iterating on prototypes, you can find me trying new coffee spots, exploring cities, or mentoring younger engineers. I believe the best designs come from living a full life outside the lab.

Why Biomedical Engineering?

From authoring a utility patent to leading FDA registration for a pediatric device, I've always aimed to create solutions that help and benefit real people — whether through rapid prototyping, human factors research, or a well-placed business case.

I love engineering solutions that sit at the edge of science and humanity.
